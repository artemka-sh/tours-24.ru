Explore Sinai Desert and a Bedouin village on ATV quad and camel ride safari starting in Sharm El Sheikh. Enjoy visiting the mountains of Echo of Sound.Head to the safari world in Sharm El sheikh to see the sunset, visit a Bedouin tent, and enjoy a delicious BBQ dinner.

Description

  • Begin your fantastic day with a hotel pickup in Sharm el-Sheikh,head straight to the quad park. Next, get a quick safety demonstration on how to ride your quad bike from your guide.
  • Ride your quad bike through Egypt’s famous Sinai desert and feel the adrenaline rush through your body. Enjoy an optional camel ride if you want to discover the wonders of the desert another way.
  • Next, visit the Bedouin village, where you can enjoy the hospitality of the Bedouins and gain an insight into the everyday lives of real desert dwellers, then have a camel ride and take unforgettable photos.
  • Have BBQ dinner and freshly-baked flatbread paired with Bedouin tea. Watch live entertainment, including a fire show and belly dance between the mountains of the Sinai Desert.
  • At the end of this fantastic trip ,you will be dropped off to your hotel in Sharm el Sheik
