Overview

Enjoy a trip by boat from Sharm El Sheikh to  White Island Ras Mohammed National Reserve with included open buffet lunch. discover the coral reef at magnificent snorkeling spots with a wide array of marine life.

We will pick up you from your hotel in Sharm El Sheikh to the marine port ,where you will board the boat to sail to Ras Mohammed National Park.

Spend a fantastic of snorkeling, there will be 3 stops in the best sites in Ras Mohammed to enjoy the coral reefs and explore the fantastic marine life in the Red Sea and stop on White Island Egyptian Maldives.

Enjoy delicious open buffet lunch on the boat. At the end of this fantastic day, you will be dropped off at your hotel.

Tour Highlights

  • Explore Ras Mohamed and White Island by boat
  • Enjoy one of the best spots for snorkeling in the Red Sea and the world
  • Have delicious open buffet lunch on the boat
  • Snorkel and swim in 3 stops in the National Park
  • Discover amazing underwater life with various fish and coral reefs
