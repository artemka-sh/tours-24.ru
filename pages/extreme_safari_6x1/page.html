Extreme Day Safari 6×1

Extreme Day Safari 6×1 several exciting entertainments are in one excursion! Quad biking, buggy and camel riding,  Bedouin tea, Echo, lunch on the beach, parachute, banana boat.

Description

  • Transfer from the hotel to the safari: around 8:30
  • ATV Quad bike 40 minutes. This is one of the most popular attractions in Sharm El Sheikh.Take amazing photos for memory, hear the Echo echoing you, visit the local residents Bedouins and taste real Bedouin tea.Adrenaline on a buggy safari 35 minutes.Camel ride 10 minutes.
  • You will experience a lot of adrenaline while riding a banana and parasailing.
  • Swimming from the sandy beach and lunch on the beach will be a great end to a day full of adventure and emotions!
  • Around 14:00, you will return to your hotel in Sharm el-Sheikh, with a lot of positive emotions, after a great day.
