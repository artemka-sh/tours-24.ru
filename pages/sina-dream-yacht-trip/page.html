Your adventure begins when you spend a day in a Luxury Sailing Boat, excursion in the Red Sea in a unique way, deluxe seafood menu, super snorkeling on the most luxury Sina Dream Yacht Trip.

We’ll pick you up from your hotel to the boat. You will receive your snorkeling equipment, and you will prepare yourself to do the first snorkeling spot, where you can explore the coral reefs and see magnificent and colorful fish.

After the first spot, lunch will be served, and relax on the boat. After lunch, stop two times for snorkeling in the different areas, where you can admire the natural reefs, beautiful corals, and colorful fish.
