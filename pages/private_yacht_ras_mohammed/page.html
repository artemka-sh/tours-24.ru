Relax in luxury as you float around the Red Sea on a Private Yacht to White Island Ras Mohammed. Go swimming, snorkel,  lie in the sun, and enjoy lunch onboard your very own boat for the day with unlimited soft drinks.

Description

  • Enjoy  from a Private Yacht to White Island Ras Mohammed Sharm El Sheikh to Ras Mohammed National Reserve with included open buffet lunch. discover the coral reef at magnificent snorkeling spots with a wide array of marine life.
  • We will pick up you from your hotel in Sharm El Sheikh to the marine port ,where you will board the boat to sail to Ras Mohammed National Park.
  • Spend a fantastic of snorkeling, there will be 3 stops in the best sites in Ras Mohammed to enjoy the coral reefs and explore the fantastic marine life in the Red Sea and white island.
  • Enjoy delicious open buffet lunch on the boat. At the end of this fantastic day, you will be dropped off at your hotel.
