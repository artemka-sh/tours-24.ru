Spend a memorable time on the water of Sharm-El-Sheikh. Participate in various water sports including parasailing, sofa, banana boat ride.

Description

Start the tour with pickup from your hotel in Sharm-El-Sheikh. Begin your experience with banana boat ride(10-15 minutes ), Next sofa (10-15 minutes) and parasailing (round on the boat 15-20 minutes flying 5-7 minutes in the air). At the end of the tour, return back to your hotel in Sharm-El-Sheikh.

Departure time & Return

  • This trip available 3 times a day at different times
  • At 10:00 am till 12:00 pm
  • At 11:00 am till 13:00 pm
  • At 14:00 pm till 16:00 pm
  • Tour available every day.
