Overview

Discover Ras Mohammed National Park on a half day trip from Sharm el-Sheikh. See Gate of God and the beautiful Mangrove trees, and swim in the clear waters of Magic Lake.

Description

  • Begin your fantastic day with a hotel pickup in Sharm el-Sheikh and enjoy the way drive to Ras Mohamed National Park. After entering the park, stop at the Gate of God, the original gateway to the park.
  • Next, head to the Suez Gulf for a swimming and snorkeling break and immerse yourself in the wonderful underwater world filled with colorful fish and coral reefs.
  • Continue your adventure through Mangrove trees sprouting from the Red Sea’s salinity-rich water. Stop at Magic Lake for a swim.
  • Enjoy the panoramic views of the sky, mountains, and sand. Return to your van and continue to Aqaba Gulf, passing by a massive earthquake crack from thousands of years ago.
  • Climb a cliff for a panoramic view of the park and snap a perfect photo before returning to your hotel.

Departure time & Return
