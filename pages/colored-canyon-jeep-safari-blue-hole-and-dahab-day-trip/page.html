Take a day tour from Sharm El Shiekh to the famous Colored Canyon, Blue Hole, Jeep Safari and Dahab. Marvel at colorful rock formations, snorkel over coral reefs, and enjoy a buffet lunch beside the sea.

Description

  • We will pick up you from your hotel in Sharm El Sheikh with air-conditioned bus and head to Dahab City for around 80km journey. Dahab is fortunate to be home to several national parks that preserve and protect the region’s natural treasures.
  • Visit the National Park of Blue Hole, relax on the beach, and enjoy an amazing experience of snorkeling and swimming on a unique and attractive coral reef spot. Watch the underwater wonderland and meet hundreds of colorful species of fish.
  • Come back to the beach camp and have your delicious barbeque lunch while watching the panoramic views of the sea and mountains. Take a sea breath, hop on a camel along the coast, and shoot remarkable photos.
  • This stop is optional: Move to the next adventure and ride a quad bike along the sea of Dahab. After boosting adrenaline with the camp activities get ready to start your next adventure.
  • Travel by jeep to the famous Canyon. Explore a vibrant labyrinth of sandstone cliffs in shades of yellow, purple, red, and gold while walking through the 800-meter gorge.
  • Then moving to the shopping center near Dahab city before coming back to Sharm El Sheikh.
