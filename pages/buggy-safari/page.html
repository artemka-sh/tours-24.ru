Explore the Sinai Desert on an exciting dune Buggy Desert Safari  trip from Sharm el-Sheikh. Soak up local culture at a Bedouin village, enjoy a traditional cup of tea, and add an optional dinner and show.

Description

  • Begin your adventure outside your hotel in Sharm el-Sheikh in an air-conditioned bus and head to a buggy station in the desert. Once at the dune buggy station, listen to a safety briefing and learn the basics of driving a dune buggy.
  • When you’re ready, hop on and enjoy a thrilling ride through the sand dunes in the Echo Mountains region of the desert. Stop in the Echo Sound region, also known as the Echo Temple, and enjoy the stunning sounds of the desert.
  • Next, head to an authentic Bedouin tent and relax with a traditional cup of tea and a warm welcome.
  • Depending on the option you select, enjoy a leisurely camel ride across the beautiful sand dunes as the sun sets over the horizon. Choose the dinner and show option to enjoy a delicious BBQ meal in a Bedouin tent.
  • As you eat, watch a sensuous Tanoura and belly dance performance, followed by a breathtaking fire show. Once the show ends, return by van to your hotel in Sharm el-Sheikh.
