Visit Mount Moses and St. Catherine Monastery in Sinai Desert and hike to the top of Mount Moses. Marvel at the incredible sunrise from the summit. Visit the oldest Christian monastery in the world — St. Catherine Monastery.

Description

  • We will pick up you from your hotel in Sharm El Sheikh to Moses Mountain in an air-conditioned bus.
  • Begin your journey to the holy site where the Prophet Moses received the Ten Commandments once you reach Mount Sinai.Ascend 750 stairs and continue your trek through the night, reaching up to 2,286 meters.
  • Take some amazing pictures or pray while admiring the sunrise, then start the ascent back down.Visit St. Catherine’s Monastery  from the 5th century, a UNESCO World Heritage Site next. Examine the ancient texts on exhibit at the library.
  • At the end of this fantastic trip ,you will be dropped off to your hotel in Sharm el Sheikh
