Seascope Sharm El-Sheikh Royal Submarine Discover the underwater wonders on a submarine cruise in Sharm El-Sheikh. Admire the underwater views from an air-conditioned seat in an observation deck 4 meters below sea level.

Description

  • Enjoy a comfortable pickup from your hotel in Sharm el-Sheikh and drive to the coast. Board the submarine and dive into the water
  • Our programme starts with first departing the marina and cruise for 15 minutes to one of the best coral reefs near the Sharm coastline. Then, descending to an air-conditioned underwater glassed-walled observation deck, view the extraordinary underwater world through huge panoramic windows from the comfort of your own seat for approximately 60 minutes. See Sharm el-Sheik’s famous and vibrant marine life from the comfort of the Seascope Sharm El-Sheikh Royal Submarine .
