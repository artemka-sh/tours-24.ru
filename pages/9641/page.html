Glass Boat from Sharm El Sheikh enjoy the fantastic underwater life of the Red Sea without getting wet! In one hour, observe different species of fish and the vibrant colors of the coral reefs. You might even find Nemo the fish! Then, upon your return, enjoy a complimentary soft drink while admiring the views from the top of the Glass Boat.
